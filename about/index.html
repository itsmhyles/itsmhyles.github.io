About Me

Hi! I'm Mhyles, a Computer Science graduate student at Boston University.

My journey began in accounting and finance, where I developed a strong foundation in analytical thinking and problem-solving. Through my work in strategy and product roles at tech startups, I discovered my passion for technology and its potential to create meaningful impact.

I write about my thoughts on technology, life, and everything in between in my blog. You can also check out my reading list, film reviews, and food adventures.

  • Data Science
  • AI Applications
  • Economic Policy Analysis

I'm always eager to connect with fellow developers, researchers, and anyone interested in the intersection of technology and economic policy. Feel free to reach out!
